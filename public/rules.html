Rules

A list of ingredients is generated

Your goal is to guess what product the ingredient list belongs to

A guess is correct if your input appears inside the product name

For example: a guess of "potato" matches "kettle cooked potato chips"

You can guess as many times as you want

Click "Give up" to reveal the answer

Click "Hint" to reveal the products category or manufacturer
