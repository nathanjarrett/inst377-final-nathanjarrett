How to Play Ingredle

A list of ingredients will be generated and your goal is to guess what food the ingredient list belongs to.

The products could be chips, candy, snacks, drinks etc. It could be generic names or name brands.

A guess is correct if your input appears inside the product name

For example: a guess of "potato" matches "kettle cooked potato chips"

You can guess as many times as you want and each correct guess adds 1 to your score

Enter your username to keep track of your score against the global competition!

Or click "Give up" to reveal the answer
